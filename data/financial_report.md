# Q3 2025 Quarterly Business Review (QBR) - Antigravity Corp

## Executive Summary
Q3 2025 was a landmark quarter for Antigravity Corp. We successfully deployed the "Agentic Reasoning Engine" to production, resulting in significant operational efficiency gains.

## Key Financials
*   **Total Revenue**: $4.85 Million (Up 12% QoQ)
*   **Gross Margin**: 78.5%
*   **Cloud Infrastructure Costs**: $42,500 (Down from $85,000 in Q2 due to local inference optimization).
*   **Net Profit**: $1.2 Million

## Operational Metrics
*   **Average API Latency**: Reduced from 1,200ms to **15ms** using ModernBERT.
*   **Hallucination Rate**: Dropped to <0.1%.
*   **Total Requests Processed**: 2.5 Million

## Strategic Outlook
We project Q4 Revenue to reach $5.2 Million. The primary focus will be scaling the "Inference Gateway" infrastructure to support 10k concurrent users.
